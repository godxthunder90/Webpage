CrustMC Rules

Follow the rules to keep gameplay fair and fun for everyone.

📌 Network Rules

  • No hacking, cheating, X-ray, or unfair clients.
  • No abusing bugs/glitches. Report them to staff.
  • No hate speech, racism, extreme toxicity, or threats.
  • No scamming or fake giveaways.
  • Respect staff decisions. Appeals can be done properly.

⚔️ Lifesteal Rules

  • No teaming in 1v1 / tournaments (if staff says).
  • No combat logging.
  • No trap-kill abusing spawn areas.
  • No alt abuse for farming hearts.
  • Be competitive but don’t be toxic.

🌿 Classic Survival Rules

  • No griefing claimed bases.
  • No stealing from claimed areas.
  • No lag machines or chunk bans.
  • No dupes or exploit farms.
  • Keep builds clean near spawn.
